Welcome to
Savoria Restaurant

Founded in 2010, Savoria was born from a simple belief — that great food should bring people together. Nestled in the heart of the city, we blend traditional recipes with modern culinary techniques to deliver an unforgettable dining experience.

Every ingredient is locally sourced, every recipe perfected over years, and every plate crafted with care. Whether it's a quiet dinner for two or a celebration with family, we make every visit special.

Meet Our Expert Chef

Chef Marco Rivera brings over 20 years of culinary mastery to every plate at Savoria. Trained in Paris and seasoned across three continents, his philosophy is simple — cook with heart, serve with pride.

  • Trained at Le Cordon Bleu, Paris
  • 20+ years of culinary experience
  • Specializes in fusion cuisine
  • Award-winning seasonal menus

  • Custom menus for private events
